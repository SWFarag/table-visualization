NastyBugs

A Simple Method for Extracting Antimicrobial Resistance Information from Metagenomes Hackathon team: Lead: Steve Tsang - SysAdmins: Greg Fedewa, Daniel Quang, Sherif Farag - Writers: Matthew Moss, Alexey V. Rakov Antibiotic resistance of bacterial pathogens remains a major threat to public health around the world. Fast and reliable extraction of antimicrobial resistance genomic signatures from large raw sequencing datasets obtained from human metagenomes is a key task for bioinformatics. NastyBugs is a versatile workflow for fast extracting of antimicrobial resistance genomic signatures from metagenomes. Objective: Create a reusable, reproducible, scalable, interoperable workflow to locate antimicrobial resistant genomic signatures in SRA shotgun sequencing (metagenomics) datasets.
